The Characters

Tha Body was a vision by myself brought to life by a hand full of close friends and music mates and my love for storytelling and animation. This really could've grown into something cool, if we had kept going.

All of the characters are portrayals of the actors' actual selves, and we had a lot of fun throwing this pilot together. This episode centers around a pet-related mishap that happens in the midst of a studio session halted by Lee's relentless writer's block.

K-DuBB

K-DuBB starts off the episode by giving the fast food restaurant, Nuthin' Butta Burger a piece of his mind as he orders his meal. They have a habit of putting onions of his burger, when he specifically tells them not to.

He's not in the best of moods, and no one really knows why, but the reason will soon unfold.

Brutha Mak

Brutha Mak is the one who is in the hot seat in this episode. He has a secret that he's been holding on to for a few days, and this studio session looks like the place where it's all gonna come out. Working at Spades Pizza has worn him out to the point of poor judgement behind the wheel, and an unaware 4-legged runaway is about to learn that the hard way.

Lee (Flud) Prince

Lee is a source of frustration to K-DuBB, because he is suffering from the most extreme case of writers block. Nothing seems to be helping him come up with the 16 bars he needs to get this time in the booth over with. No worries though, because he's about to get for material that he can fit into a day's session when Mak makes it to the studio and shakes things up.

K-DuBB's Missing Dog

Part-Way through this Episode, we find out why K-DuBB is so upset. He hasn't seen his dog in days, and he's worried that the rottweiler and chihuahua mix he loves so much might not ever come home. Due to this frustration, he has been a pain to work with in the studio.

JD's Failed Trip to the Studio

JD's day is going much better. While he has no missing pet tp potentially grieve, his plans of productivity are dashed when we realizes that he left his house with the doors locked and his wife outside. With his day totally shot, he must fight the traffic back home to fix what he messed up.

JD's Wife Locked Outside

JD's wife impatiently waits at home for him to come unlock the door and is undoubtedly adding this to the list along with him tracking dirt across the floor after cutting the grass and leaving a pile of clothes in the middle of the floor.

It's a Comedic Lesson in Forgiveness

When K-DuBB realizes what actually happened to his dog, he and Brutha Mak have it out in the studio, but you'll have to watch for yourself to see how it ends.
